Connect Your Discord App

To connect your Discord application:

  1. Visit the Discord Developer Portal
  2. Select your application or create a new one
  3. Navigate to the "OAuth2" section
  4. Configure your redirect URIs and copy your Client ID
  5. Use the configuration pages in this dashboard to set up your endpoints
Security Notice: Never share your Client Secret or Bot Token publicly. All secrets should be stored securely on your server.
